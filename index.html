About Us

Goldhorn Hotel and Banquets, Mehdipatnam

Welcome to Goldhorn Hotel and Banquets, located in the heart of Hyderabad. Our 3 star hotel offers a perfect blend of modern amenities and traditional hospitality.

Since opening in 2017, our hotel has been offering a variety of rooms crafted for both comfort and convenience. Each room is equipped with modern amenities such as air conditioning, high speed Wi-Fi, comfortable workspaces and private bathrooms with premium toiletries. Our rooms feature plush bedding and a peaceful ambiance ensuring you a restful stay.
Our goal is to offer a serene ambiance where every guest can enjoy comfort and convenience in a vibrant city.

At Goldhorn Banquets, we specialize in crafting unforgettable events. Our three elegant banquet halls, including a dedicated dining hall with a fully equipped kitchen are perfect for a variety of occasions that are ranging from intimate family gatherings to large weddings and corporate meetings. With a capacity of up to 250 guests, each hall is equipped with state of the art audiovisual technology, customizable décor options and a professional team to ensure your event is executed flawlessly.
